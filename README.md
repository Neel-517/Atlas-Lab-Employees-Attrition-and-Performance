# Atlas Lab Employees Attrition and Performance

## [Tableau Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/views/AtlasLabemployees/Demographic?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)

## **Project** 

This analysis dives into detailed data on Atlas Lab employees. Focusing on employee demographics, job breakdown, and rating of employees who gave a score of 1 through 5 on a 5-point scale, which includes environment satisfaction, job satisfaction, work-life balance, and relationship satisfaction. The objective is to identify issues in areas that may be correlated with turnover.
