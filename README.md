This analysis dives into detailed data on Atlas Lab employees. Focusing on employee demogrpahics, job breakdown, and rating of employees who gave a score of 1 or 2 on a 5-point scale, which include environment satisfaction, job satisfaction, work-life balance, and relationship satisfaction. The objective is to find problems in areas that may correlate with turnover and burnout risk.
